Meta Python Course

learn python.coursera

Spent the summer learning and analyzing what Python is using the META provided course Some key takeaways Type Casting & Data Types: Learning how to use implicit and explicit casting to ensure accurate data handling, with experience in manipulating strings, integers, and floats. Flow Control & Loops: Practical experience with control flow structures like if-else, elif, for loops, and while loops, enabling dynamic decision-making and efficient iteration over data. Functions & Scope: Understanding how Python functions work with different scopes (local, global, enclosing) to manage variables and logic effectively. Algorithms: Gaining knowledge in designing step-by-step algorithms to solve problems and implement Pythonic solutions. Light Up Project

https://linktr.ee/lightuproject

Light Up Project, is a student-led mental health organization that aims to destigmatize mental health through peer support, peer connection, and education. Light Up has five main sections: the Column, Art Up and Events, Team Social Media, and the Bring New Podcast Team. The Column publishes original articles our members wrote about mental health; Art Up creates illustrations; Events organizes fundraisers, workshops, and other events worldwide. The Podcast team is our new department, making podcasts to spread awareness, and we are on Spotify. Team Social Media is our current most notable team. Our team, Instagram, makes posts with averages of 100-150 likes per post and a following of over one thousand across 50 and more posts.
